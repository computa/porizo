# Porizo Social Content Strategy — 2026-04-09

## The Core Insight

Porizo's product IS the content. Every song created is a shareable emotional moment. The strategy isn't "make content about the app" — it's "capture the moments the app already creates."

The viral loop: **someone sees a reaction video** → **downloads Porizo** → **makes a song** → **records the reaction** → **posts it** → repeat.

---

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok (Primary — discovery engine)

TikTok's algorithm rewards novel, emotional, short-form content from day one regardless of follower count. Porizo songs are exactly this.

**Why TikTok first:**
- Algorithm-driven discovery (followers don't matter early on)
- Emotional/surprise content performs exceptionally well
- Sound is native to the platform (songs play in-feed)
- Duet/Stitch features enable organic UGC amplification
- Voice AI is a trending topic with built-in curiosity

### Instagram Reels (Secondary — conversion engine)

Reels for reach, Stories for engagement, bio link for conversion. Cross-post TikTok content with minor adaptation.

**Why secondary:**
- Algorithm favors established accounts more than TikTok
- Better for retargeting and nurturing (Stories, DMs)
- Slightly older demo (25-45) aligns with gift-giving occasions
- Carousel posts for tutorials/explainers

## Content Pillars

### 1. Reaction Videos (60% of content) — THE GROWTH ENGINE

The money shot. Someone hears a song made for them and reacts emotionally.

**Formats:**
- "I made my mom a song in MY voice for Mother's Day" → reaction
- "My wife didn't know AI could clone my voice" → her reaction
- "Surprising my best friend with a birthday song" → reaction
- Split-screen: phone creating song on left, reaction on right
- POV: "When your partner sends you a song and it's THEIR voice"

**Why this works:**
- Emotional payoff in <30 seconds
- Immediately communicates what the app does
- Creates FOMO ("I want someone to make one for ME")
- Every reaction is unique — infinite content variety

**Production notes:**
- Film vertically (9:16), natural lighting
- Capture the FIRST listen — can't fake genuine surprise
- Include 2-3 seconds of the actual song playing
- End with app name/CTA overlay

### 2. Creation Process (20% of content) — DEMYSTIFIES THE PRODUCT

Show how stupidly easy it is. Type a message, pick a style, hear yourself singing.

**Formats:**
- Screen recording: "3 steps to make a birthday song in your voice" (30s)
- Time-lapse: blank screen → finished song playing in <15s
- "Watch me make a [occasion] song in 60 seconds"
- Side-by-side: your normal speaking voice vs. the song output
- "POV: You just discovered you can make yourself sing"

**Why this works:**
- Removes "is this hard?" objection
- The voice transformation is visually/aurally surprising
- Shows the product without feeling like an ad

### 3. Occasion Hooks (15% of content) — SEASONAL RELEVANCE

Tie content to upcoming occasions. Creates urgency and relevance.

**Calendar (next 90 days):**

| Date | Occasion | Content Angle |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Apr 20 | Easter | "Easter surprise song for the family" |
| May 4 | Star Wars Day | Fun/novelty song, trending sounds |
| May 11 | Mother's Day | "The gift she'll actually cry about" |
| Jun 15 | Father's Day | "Dad's never heard himself sing" |
| Jun-Aug | Wedding Season | "Custom first dance songs" |
| Ongoing | Birthdays | Evergreen — "Better than a card" |

**Why this works:**
- People actively search for gift ideas before occasions
- Creates urgency ("Mother's Day is in 3 days")
- Hashtag riding (#mothersday, #birthdaygift)

### 4. Behind the Scenes / Educational (5% of content) — TRUST & BRAND

Light touches. Not too much — people care about output, not process.

**Formats:**
- "How AI voice cloning actually works (in 30 seconds)"
- "We built an app that makes you sing" — founder story
- "The tech behind personalized songs" — keep it accessible
- Responding to comments/questions

## Content Production Pipeline

### Weekly Cadence

| Day | TikTok | Instagram |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Mon | Reaction video | Cross-post Reel + Story poll |
| Tue | — | Carousel: "5 occasions for a Porizo song" |
| Wed | Creation process | Cross-post Reel |
| Thu | Reaction video | Story: behind-the-scenes |
| Fri | Occasion/trending hook | Cross-post Reel |
| Sat | — | Story: user reshare / poll |
| Sun | — | — |

**Target: 3 TikToks/week, 3 Reels/week (mostly cross-posted), 3-4 Stories/week**

This is sustainable for a solo founder. Quality > quantity at this stage.

### Production Workflow

```
1. CAPTURE
   - Record reactions as they happen (always have phone ready)
   - Screen-record song creation demos
   - Save best songs for content use

2. EDIT (same day, <30 min per piece)
   - CapCut (free, TikTok-native editing)
   - Add captions (CRITICAL — 80%+ watch muted on IG)
   - Add hook text in first 2 seconds
   - Trim to <45s for TikTok, <60s for Reels

3. POST
   - TikTok: post natively (algorithm penalizes cross-posted watermarks)
   - Instagram: re-export from CapCut without TikTok watermark
   - Add 3-5 relevant hashtags (not 30)
   - Pin comment with App Store link

4. ENGAGE (15 min/day)
   - Reply to every comment in first hour
   - Engage with similar creators
   - Duet/Stitch relevant content
```

### Batch Production (Monthly)

Once a month, batch-record 4-6 reaction videos:
1. Ask friends/family to let you record their reaction
2. Create songs in advance for various occasions
3. Film all reactions in one session
4. Edit and schedule throughout the month

This creates a content buffer so you're never scrambling.

## Seeding Strategy (First 30 Days)

You can't wait for organic growth from zero. Seed the flywheel:

### Week 1-2: Friends & Family Reactions
- Make songs for 10 people you know
- Record their genuine first-listen reactions
- Post the best 5 as your initial content
- Ask them to share on their own accounts

### Week 2-3: Micro-Creator Outreach
- Find 10-20 creators in the "gifts" / "relationships" / "family" niche
- Offer them free premium access
- Ask them to make a song for someone and film the reaction
- No script — authentic reactions perform better than scripted

### Week 3-4: Comment Seeding
- Comment genuinely on viral "gift idea" and "birthday surprise" videos
- "This reminds me of when I made my mom a song in my own voice and she cried"
- NOT "check out our app" — that gets shadowbanned
- Profile link does the conversion work

### Ongoing: UGC Amplification
- Create a branded hashtag: #PorizoSong or #MadeWithPorizo
- Reshare any user content with permission
- Feature best reactions weekly

## Metrics to Track

### Vanity (awareness):
- Follower growth rate
- Total video views
- Profile visits

### Signal (intent):
- App Store impressions from "social media" source
- Link clicks from bio
- Saves and shares per video (saves = high intent)

### Business (revenue):
- Downloads attributed to social
- Registration rate from social traffic
- Song creation rate from social-acquired users

**Review weekly. Double down on what works. Kill what doesn't.**

## Technical Enablers (Future)

These are product features that would amplify social content:

1. **In-app "Share to TikTok/IG"** — One-tap export with song clip + branded overlay
2. **Reaction recording mode** — Built-in camera that records while song plays (split-screen)
3. **Song preview cards** — Rich link previews when sharing URLs on social
4. **Referral tracking** — Attribute downloads to specific content/creators
5. **Auto-generated clips** — 15-second best-moment clips for easy sharing

These are NOT needed to start. Start with manual content creation NOW.
