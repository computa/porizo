# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Splash
Animated launch screen with particle globe visualization and mic icon — establishes brand identity while app loads.

### 2. Onboarding
Three-page wizard introducing the app's value: AI-generated personalized songs, emotional moments made musical, easy creation flow. Shown only on first launch.

### 3. Create
The heart of the app: tabbed interface (Simple/Advanced/Your Voice) for creating personalized songs — guiding users through recipient details, occasion, style selection, and AI generation. Voice enrollment is optional via "Your Voice" tab.

### 4. Songs
Library of all songs created with modern card layout, playback controls, sharing options, and variation creation. Empty state guides users to create their first song.

### 5. Poems
Library of all poems created, with viewing, sharing, and variation options.

### 6. Explore
Discover inspiration through sections (Fresh Hits, Top Songs, Popular) — browse trending creations and templates to jumpstart your own songs.

### 7. Settings
Profile management, optional voice enrollment, notification preferences, subscription management, and help/support.

## Future Explorations

Ideas for post-MVP features worth investigating:

### User Theme Customization
Allow users to choose/customize their accent color. Since this is a "love and friendship" app, different users may associate different colors with love and warmth (rose, coral, lavender, etc.). Could offer:
- Preset theme options (Rose, Coral, Lavender, Teal)
- Custom accent color picker
- Light/dark mode toggle
- Per-song color themes that match the occasion (birthday = gold, anniversary = rose, etc.)
