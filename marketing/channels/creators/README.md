# Creators and Partners

Creator and partner outreach should borrow trust from people whose audience already cares about gifts, family, relationships, parents, weddings, birthdays, or distance.

Current rule: do not DM creators with an abstract app pitch. Send one strong proof asset and offer to make a free song for one named person.
