# Marketing Strategy

- [`achieved/`](achieved/) — strategies that were tried or completed, with outcome notes.

The active plan is [`current/proof-first-distribution-reset.md`](current/proof-first-distribution-reset.md).

Older strategies are not trash; they are learning artifacts. Move them to `achieved/` with a short summary of what was tried, what happened, and what should not be repeated.
