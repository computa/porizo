## Writing Rules

- Answer the target query in the first paragraph.
- Use one primary keyword phrase consistently and naturally.
- Include real external citations, not just brand assertions.
- Prefer evidence, mechanisms, scripts, and examples over inspiration.
- Use `##` headings generously so the article is easy to scan.
- Include at least one list and one FAQ section.
- Add at least one relevant internal Porizo link if the article is on-site.
- If the topic involves safety, abuse, health, legal, or financial risk, draw a clear boundary.
- Do not let the CTA dominate the article.
- Do not publish until the deterministic review gate is approved.
