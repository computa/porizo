## CRITICAL DON'TS

- **Don't film in selfie mode** (back camera quality is meaningfully better)
- **Don't add captions/text in TikTok itself** (use CapCut, then upload — TikTok-added text gets downranked when reshared)
- **Don't use viral-sound-of-the-day trending audio** (the song IS the audio; don't compete with it)
- **Don't include a price** in video — kills the emotional moment
- **Don't watermark TikTok videos and reupload to Shorts/Reels** — algorithms detect and downrank. Always native upload.
- **Don't say "buy now" or "limited time"** — algorithms throttle hard-sell language
