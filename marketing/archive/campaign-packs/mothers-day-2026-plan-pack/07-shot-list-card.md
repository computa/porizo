## SHOOT-DAY CHECKLIST (work top-down)

- [ ] iPhone clean, fully charged, vertical mode locked
- [ ] Tripod or prop set behind/to the side of recipient — NOT in front
- [ ] Window or lamp on recipient's side (face lit, not silhouetted)
- [ ] Phone on Do Not Disturb (no notifications mid-take)
- [ ] AirPlane mode OFF (so song can stream from porizo.co share link)
- [ ] Test play the song once on a different device — confirm it works
- [ ] Get verbal consent ON camera before each take
- [ ] Record 5 seconds of silence at the start of each take (gives breathing room for editing)

## EDITING (CapCut, ~30 min/video)

- [ ] Trim to target length (don't pad — under 60s always wins)
- [ ] Hook in first 1.5s — no slow build, no cold open
- [ ] Text overlays at exact timestamps from this card
- [ ] Captions burned in (don't rely on platform auto-captions)
- [ ] No background music — the song IS the soundtrack
- [ ] Keep audio volume hot but not clipped
- [ ] End frame: solid color, text only, 5s minimum (lets repeat-viewers scan)

## CRITICAL DON'TS

- ❌ Don't film selfie mode (back camera quality is meaningfully better)
- ❌ Don't add text/stickers in TikTok itself — bake them in CapCut, then upload
- ❌ Don't watermark TikTok videos and reupload to Shorts/Reels (algorithms downrank)
- ❌ Don't include a price in video — kills the emotional moment
- ❌ Don't talk over the recipient's reaction — silence is the gift
