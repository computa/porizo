FAQ

Father's Day Song Maker questions.

How is this different from a card or a tie?

A card sits in a drawer. A tie hangs in the closet. A song stays on his phone — replayed on the bus, played for friends, kept for the years he wants to remember this Father's Day.

Will it actually feel personal?

Yes — the lyrics come from the specific memory you share about Dad, so it's unmistakably about him. You choose the musical style — folk, country, pop, R&B — and an AI vocalist performs it. That's what turns "thoughtful" into "he will keep it forever."

Can I make a Father's Day song for a stepdad or grandfather?

Absolutely. The lyrics are built from your memory of him, not a template. Whatever role he played, Porizo writes a song specific to that relationship.

How long does it take?

Preview in under 90 seconds; full 45–90 second song in about three minutes. Plenty of time to make it on Father's Day morning.

Can siblings collaborate on a song for Dad?

Yes. Each sibling can contribute a memory; Porizo weaves them into one song. Share-with-anyone (Pro plan) lets the whole family play the same link.

What if Dad does not show emotion easily?

The song is short, specific, and shared as a link he can open privately. Most "doesn't want anything" dads keep the song.
