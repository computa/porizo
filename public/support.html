Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a song?

Tap "Create Song," pick an occasion, enter who it's for, and share a memory or message. Choose a musical style and we'll generate a personalized song in minutes.

How long does it take to create a song?

Preview songs render in about 90 seconds. Full songs take 2-3 minutes.

Can I add my own voice to a song?

Yes! Voice enrollment is optional. Go to Settings → Your Voice → Set Up and record 6-8 short phrases in a quiet environment. It takes about 2 minutes.

Can I re-record my voice?

Yes! Go to Settings → Voice Profile → Re-enroll to start fresh.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes all your data including voice profiles and songs.
