Why Porizo for birthdays

A birthday gift built around them, not a category.

Most birthday gifts say "I remembered." A Porizo song says "I remembered this about you." That difference is the gift. The funny story everyone retells, the nickname only family uses, the words you never quite say out loud — those become the heart of the lyrics.

Porizo is the personalized-song gift app built around your own voice. Among the established human-composer marketplaces (Songfinch, Songlorious, Songheart, ForeverSong) none offer voice cloning of the gifter. The recipient hears the song, recognizes your voice, and the moment becomes something neither of you forgets. Available on the Plus and Pro plans after a one-time voice enrollment.

Best for

Birthday moments that earn their own song.

  • Milestone birthdays. 18, 21, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70 — the years where a card is not enough.
  • Last-minute birthdays. A finished song in about three minutes, shareable as a link the same hour.
  • First birthdays apart. A friend who moved cities, a parent overseas, a partner deployed. The song crosses the distance instantly.

  • Birthdays for kids. A song with their name, their nickname, the things they love. Lasts longer than any toy.
  • Quiet birthdays. The friend who hates a fuss but deserves a song they can replay alone.

FAQ

Birthday song questions.

How fast is "fast"? I need this for tonight.

Preview in under 90 seconds. Full 45–90 second song in about three minutes. You can have a finished personalized song before the candles are lit.

Will the song actually sound like me singing?

Yes — voice cloning is included on the Plus and Pro plans. Record 6–10 short phrases once inside the app and Porizo will sing every future song in your own voice. This is the feature that makes a birthday song land harder than any card.

Can I make a birthday song for someone who is not into music?

Yes. The song is short (under 90 seconds), specific to them, and shared as a link they can open once and never feel obligated to revisit. Most "not really a music person" recipients keep it anyway.

Can I write the lyrics myself?

You can hand Porizo as much detail as you want — a memory, a phrase, a poem you wrote, a bullet list of inside jokes. Porizo turns it into structured verse / chorus / bridge lyrics in the genre you pick.

How does this compare to Songfinch for a birthday gift?

Songfinch uses human composers and ships in around 4–7 days for ~$179.99–$199.99 (rush options extra). Excellent if the birthday is two weeks out. Porizo ships in minutes for $9.99/month and adds voice cloning, which Songfinch cannot do. Different tools for different birthdays.

Can the recipient share the song with their friends?

Yes — by default. The Pro plan unlocks "share with anyone" so they can post it to their story, send to friends, or replay it on any device.
