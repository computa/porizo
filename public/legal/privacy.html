Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Porizo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Voice Recordings and Audio Data

To create personalized songs that sound like you, we collect:

  • Voice enrollment recordings: 6-8 short phrases you record during the voice enrollment process (typically 2-3 minutes total)
  • Voice embeddings: A 256-dimensional mathematical representation of your voice characteristics (this is non-invertible and cannot be used to recreate your voice recordings)
Important: Raw voice recordings are automatically deleted from our servers after 7 days. Voice embeddings are retained until you delete your account.

1.2 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address, name (from OAuth provider)
  • Phone number: If you choose phone authentication
  • OAuth data: Information provided by Apple, Google, or Facebook when you sign in with these services

1.3 Song Creation Data

When you create songs, we collect:

  • Recipient information: Name and relationship of the person the song is for
  • Personal messages: The custom message or story you provide for song lyrics
  • Occasion details: The type of occasion (birthday, anniversary, etc.)
  • Style preferences: Musical genre and style choices

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers: App-scoped device identifier stored in iOS Keychain for secure device binding
  • IP address: Used for fraud prevention and security monitoring, and may be hashed in audit logs
  • Usage data: Features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Audio cache: Recently played songs cached locally for up to 7 days

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • In transit: All data is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens: Stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • Offline cache: Audio files encrypted with AES keys stored in Keychain
  • Voice embeddings: Encrypted at rest using user-specific keys
  • Audio watermarking: All generated songs contain metadata watermarks for content verification

5. Data Retention

Local audio cache 7 days (on device)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke permissions at any time through device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@porizo.co

7. Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Voice embeddings are permanently deleted
  • All generated songs are removed
  • Personal information is erased
  • Share links become invalid

This process is irreversible. Account deletion can be initiated from Settings within the app or by contacting support.

8. Share Links and Device Binding

When you share a song:

  • A unique, one-time share link is generated
  • The link binds to the first app device that opens it (using an app-scoped device identifier)
  • This prevents unauthorized redistribution of your personalized content
  • Share links can be revoked at any time from the app

9. Risk Scoring and Automated Actions

To prevent misuse of our voice cloning technology:

  • We maintain a risk score for each account (0-100)
  • Scores are based on usage patterns and content moderation flags
  • High-risk accounts may have voice features disabled
  • You can appeal automated decisions by contacting support

10. Children's Privacy

Porizo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@porizo.co.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers of personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us
