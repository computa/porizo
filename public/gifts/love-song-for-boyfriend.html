FAQ

Love-song-for-boyfriend questions.

What if we've only been together a few months?

Porizo works for any stage. Give it one specific moment from those months — a first impression that stuck, an early inside joke. The lyric grows from that detail. Short relationships are some of the best song subjects because the details are fresh.

How do I choose how the song sounds?

You pick the music style — acoustic, pop, R&B, country, indie folk — and an AI vocalist performs the lyrics built from your memory. For a love song to a partner, the specific moment is half the gift.

What if he's not really 'into' love songs?

Most boyfriends who 'don't really listen to that stuff' play these anyway. The reason is the specificity. It's not a love song — it's a song about him.

Can I make it funny or playful instead of romantic?

Yes. Porizo lets you pick the genre and tone — country, pop, R&B, indie folk, even something more upbeat. The lyric tone follows your direction.

Should I share it on social or send it privately?

Most people send it privately first. He gets to hear it alone, react alone, and decide if he wants to share. Some couples post it later as their anniversary post — that part is his call.

How is this different from a Spotify playlist for him?

A playlist says 'I curated these.' A Porizo song says 'this exists nowhere else — it was written for you, sung by me, three minutes ago.' Different category of gift entirely.
