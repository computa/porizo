Why Porizo for stepdad's Father's Day

The song says the thing 'Happy Father's Day' can't.

Stepdads sit in an awkward seat on Father's Day. A generic card feels too small. A heartfelt note feels too vulnerable to hand over. A Porizo song does what a card can't: it names the moment he earned the title — the day he showed up at the school play, the first time he called you on your birthday without being prompted, the way he handles your mom when she's tired. FAQ

Stepdad Father's Day song questions.

Is it weird to give my stepdad a Father's Day song?

If you've been thinking about whether to acknowledge him on Father's Day, that thought is already the answer. The song lets you say what feels too vulnerable in a card. Most stepdads keep these gifts permanently.

What if our relationship is complicated?

The lyric works from whatever you give it. You don't have to pretend things are simpler than they are. Many of the most-played Porizo songs for stepdads are about the specific way he showed up — not about the bigger relationship.

Can I make it sound like me singing?

Yes — voice cloning is included on Plus and Pro. After a one-time voice enrollment, Porizo sings every song in your own voice. Hearing your voice is part of what makes this land for him.

How quickly can I do this — Father's Day is days away?

Preview in under 90 seconds. Full song in about three minutes. You can finish the song this morning and send it before dinner.

Should I send it to him privately or play it at the family gathering?

Most people send it privately first — by text, by email, by a quiet moment after dessert — so he can hear it alone before he has to react in front of anyone. Then play it later if the moment fits.

What if I want to include something about him being a grandfather to my kids?

Mention it in the memory you give Porizo. The lyric will weave both layers — what he was to you and what he is to your kids — into the same song.
