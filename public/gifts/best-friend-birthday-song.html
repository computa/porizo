What if my best friend isn't into emotional gifts?

Porizo lets you pick the tone. A funny, upbeat 60-second song about your worst inside joke lands better for many friendships than a serious ballad. The genre and tone follow your lead.

How do I choose how the song sounds?

You pick the music style — indie folk, pop, country, hip-hop — and an AI vocalist performs the lyrics built from your memory. The shared history in the lyric is what makes a best-friend song work.

Can I make it funny instead of sentimental?

Absolutely. Pick a genre that fits the friendship — country storytelling, pop, hip-hop, indie. The lyric tone follows.

What if we live in different countries?

What if I want to play it at the party?
