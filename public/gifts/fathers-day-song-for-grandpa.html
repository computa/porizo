FAQ

Grandpa Father's Day song questions.

Will my grandfather know how to play a web link?

Open the link on your phone, hand him the phone, hit play. The song plays in the browser — no app, no install, no account. If he's playing voice messages on the family group chat, he'll handle this fine.

Can the grandkids' names be in the song?

Yes — include them in the memory you describe. The lyric will weave names into the verse naturally.

What if my grandfather is hard of hearing?

Porizo lets you preview and re-render the song with different genres. Slower acoustic styles tend to be easier to follow. You can also share the lyric text alongside the audio.

How long is the song? Can it be longer for a milestone?

Songs are 45–90 seconds. For an 80th or 90th birthday Father's Day, the under-90-seconds length actually works in your favor — long enough to land, short enough to play three times in a row.

Can the grandkid be the one giving the song?

Yes. Whoever sets it up describes the memory and picks the music style — so a grandkid can build a song from their own moment with grandpa. An AI vocalist performs the lyrics in the style you choose.
