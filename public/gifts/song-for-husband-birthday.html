What if his birthday is today?

Preview in under 90 seconds. Full song in about three minutes. You can finish the song in the time it takes him to find his keys.

Will the song be in my voice?

Yes — voice cloning is on Plus and Pro. Record 6–10 short phrases once and every song is in your voice. For a husband's birthday song, your voice is the part that makes it stick.

He says he doesn't want gifts. Will he like this?

Yes. Husbands who refuse gifts almost always keep these songs. The reason is it's not a thing — it's a moment. He's not turning down a moment.

Can I include our kids' names or our pets?

Yes. Mention them in the memory you describe. The lyric weaves them in naturally.
