What if he doesn't really like Valentine's Day?

Most men who 'don't really do Valentine's' play these anyway. The reason is it's not a Valentine's gift — it's a song about him that happened to arrive on February 14. Different category.

Yes — voice cloning is on Plus and Pro. Record 6–10 short phrases once and every song is in your own voice. For a Valentine's song to a husband or boyfriend, your voice is what makes it land.

Yes. Mention them in the memory you describe. The lyric weaves them in.
