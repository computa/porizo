FAQ

Graduation Song Maker questions.

What detail should I share about the graduate?

Concrete over abstract. The major, the school, the late-night habit, the time they almost quit, the friend who got them through. Porizo expands the detail into structured lyrics that name the specific story.

Will the song sound like me singing it?

Yes — voice cloning is on the Plus and Pro plans. After a one-time enrollment inside the app, every song is sung in your own voice. For a parent's gift to a graduating child, this is the feature that lands hardest.

Can I play it at the graduation party?

Yes — every song generates a shareable link that plays in any browser, plus a downloadable file. Connect to a Bluetooth speaker and play it during the toast.

How long does it take?

Preview in under 90 seconds; full song in about three minutes. Make it the morning of graduation.

Can multiple family members contribute?

Yes. Pool memories — what Mom remembers, what siblings remember, what the grandparents want to say. Porizo weaves them into one cohesive song.

Can I order multiple songs for several graduates?

Yes. The Plus plan includes 4 songs/month and Pro includes 10 — perfect for a family with multiple graduates this year.
