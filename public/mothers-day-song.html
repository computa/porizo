Why a song for Mom

A gift she will replay, not just unwrap.

A bouquet wilts. A card sits in a drawer. A song stays on her phone — replayed on the bus, played for friends, kept for the years she wants to remember this Mother's Day. Porizo turns one real memory into an original song built around the small detail no other gift can touch: the way she said something, the meal she always made, the phrase that meant home.

Porizo is the personalized-song gift app built around your own voice. Among the established human-composer marketplaces (Songfinch, Songlorious, Songheart, ForeverSong) none offer voice cloning of the gifter. That detail flips the entire moment — the song stops feeling like a product and starts feeling like a letter set to music. Most moms cry the first time they hear it. That is the gift.

FAQ

Mother's Day song questions.

How long does it take to make a Mother's Day song?

Under 90 seconds for a 15–25 second preview. About three minutes for the full 45–90 second song. You can preview multiple genres before committing — perfect when Mother's Day is days away.

Will it really sound like me singing it?

Yes — voice cloning is included on the Plus and Pro plans. After a one-time enrollment (record 6–10 short phrases inside the app), Porizo sings the song in your own voice. No other personalized-song service offers this in 2026.

Does Mom need to install anything to hear it?

No. Every song produces a shareable link that plays instantly in her browser. She can replay it as many times as she wants. If she does install the app, the song is also saved to her library.

How is this different from Songfinch or Songlorious for Mother's Day?

Songfinch and Songlorious match you with a human composer who delivers in days (Songfinch ~4–7 days standard) for ~$179.99–$199.99 per song. Porizo delivers in minutes for $9.99/month and adds voice cloning. If Mother's Day is more than two weeks away and you want a human-composed song, Songfinch is excellent. If you need something today — or you want it to sound like you singing — Porizo is built for that.

What if I want to redo a section?

Re-rolls cost less than a fresh song. You can regenerate just the lyrics, just the beat, just the vocal tone, or just one section (verse / chorus / bridge) until it lands. Most users find the right version in two attempts.

Can I make a song for a step-mom, foster mom, or grandmother?

Absolutely. The lyrics are built from the memory you share, not from any template. Whatever role she played, Porizo writes a song specific to that relationship and that memory.
