FAQ

Birthday Song for Mom questions.

How is this different from a Mother's Day song?

Mother's Day is a holiday on the calendar; her birthday is just hers. The song you make for her birthday should reference the year she was born, the milestone she's hitting, the things only this birthday means. Porizo lets you tell that specific story, not a generic Mom story.

Will the song actually feel personal?

Yes — the lyrics come from the specific memory you share about her, so it's unmistakably about her, not a generic Mom song. You choose the musical style and an AI vocalist performs it. For a child gifting a parent, that specificity is what turns "thoughtful" into "she will cry."

What if I do not know what to write?

Share one detail — a phrase she always says, a meal she always made, a sacrifice she made you noticed years later. Porizo expands the detail into structured verse / chorus / bridge lyrics in the genre you choose. Specific beats general — every time.

How long does it take?

Preview in under 90 seconds; full 45–90 second song in about three minutes. You can re-roll the lyrics, the beat, or just one section until it lands. Most users find the right version in two attempts.

Can siblings collaborate on a song for Mom?

Yes. Each sibling can contribute a memory; Porizo weaves them into one song. Pro plan unlocks share-with-anyone so the whole family can play the same link.

How does this compare to a card or a bouquet?

A card sits in a drawer. A bouquet wilts. A song stays on her phone — replayed on the bus, played for friends, kept for the years she wants to remember this birthday. Different gift, different lifespan.
