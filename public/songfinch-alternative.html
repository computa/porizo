When Porizo beats Songfinch.

FAQ

Songfinch vs Porizo questions.

Is Porizo cheaper than Songfinch?

Yes. Porizo Plus is $9.99/month for 4 songs (about $2.50/song); Pro is $14.99/month for 10 songs (about $1.50/song). Songfinch starts around $179.99 per song. The cost models are different — Porizo is a subscription with multi-song output; Songfinch is per-song with a human composer.

Is Porizo faster than Songfinch?

Yes — by orders of magnitude. Porizo previews in under 90 seconds and finishes the full song in about three minutes. Songfinch standard delivery is around 4–7 days, with paid rush options. If your gift is days away, Porizo is the option built for that timeline.

Will a Porizo song actually feel personal?

Yes. The lyrics are built from the specific memory you share, so the song is unmistakably about your person. You pick the music style — pop, country, R&B, Afropop, folk, acoustic — and an AI vocalist performs it. That specificity is what Songfinch charges a human composer and a week to deliver; Porizo does it in minutes.

When should I still use Songfinch instead of Porizo?

Use Songfinch when you want a real human composer's interpretation, you have at least a week, and budget for ~$179.99–$199.99 per song (more with rush). Use Porizo when you need it in hours, you want it for about $9, or you want multiple songs across the year on a subscription.

Is the music quality the same?

Different, not lower. Songfinch uses real human composers and artists. Porizo uses AI music generation (Suno) and AI voice conversion (Seed-VC). The result is original lyrics and original music for both. Porizo's quality control is section-by-section so the chorus, verse, and bridge each pass before the song is delivered.

Do recipients need to install anything?

No. Porizo songs ship with a shareable web link that plays instantly in any browser. Songfinch delivers an MP3 file. Both can be saved and replayed.
