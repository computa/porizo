Why a custom song

A gift built around them, not a category.

Most gifts are picked from a shelf. A custom song is built from one detail only you would know — the nickname, the moment, the line they always say. That detail becomes the heart of the lyrics. The result is impossible to mistake for anyone else's gift.

Porizo is the personalized-song gift app built around one real detail from your story. The established human-composer marketplaces (Songfinch, Songlorious, Songheart, ForeverSong) take days and cost up to ~$299 per song. Porizo previews in about 90 seconds, delivers the full song in minutes for around $9, and lets you pick the musical style. The recipient hears a song that names something only the two of you would know, and the moment becomes something neither of you forgets.

Best for

Moments a generic gift cannot reach.

  • Thank-yous. A friend who showed up. A teacher who changed everything. A sibling who carried more than their share.

FAQ

Custom song questions.

What kinds of memories work best as inputs?

Specific over general. "She always brings extra napkins" is better than "she's thoughtful." A nickname, a phrase, a meal, a date — anything concrete. Porizo expands the detail into structured lyrics.

Will it actually feel personal?

Yes — the lyrics come from the specific detail you share, so it's unmistakably about the person you're making it for. You choose the musical style and an AI vocalist performs it. That specificity is what turns "thoughtful" into "I'll keep this forever."

How long does the song take?

Preview in under 90 seconds. Full 45–90 second song in about three minutes. You can re-roll lyrics, beat, vocal, or just one section until the song lands.

Can I make a custom song for any occasion?

Yes. Birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, Mother's Day, Father's Day, graduations, thank-yous, apologies, remembrance — and "just because" days that deserved a song without a holiday attached.

Can the recipient share the song with others?

By default, the link is theirs to play. The Pro plan unlocks "share with anyone" so they can post it to their story or send to friends.

How does this compare to Songfinch, Songlorious, or Songheart?

Those services use human composers and ship in 24 hours to 30 days depending on tier for ~$69.99–$299 per song. Porizo uses AI, previews in about 90 seconds, and ships the full song in minutes for $9.99/month — and you pick the musical style. Different tools for different timelines and budgets.
