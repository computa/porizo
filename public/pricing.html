Questions?

Can I try Porizo before subscribing?

Absolutely. The free tier gives you 1 song and 1 poem — no credit card, no strings.

What happens to my songs if I cancel?

Your songs are yours forever. Canceling stops future renewals but never takes away what you've already created.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade right in the app under Settings. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

How does annual billing work?

You pay once for the full year and save ~17% compared to monthly. The full amount is charged upfront.

What is voice cloning?

Record your voice once in the app and every song will sound like you singing. It takes about 2 minutes to set up.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
