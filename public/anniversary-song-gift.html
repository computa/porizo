FAQ

Anniversary song questions.

How specific can the lyrics get?

Very. Share the city, the date, the meal, the phrase you say to each other. Porizo writes lyrics that name the specific detail — a generic love song is what we are avoiding.

Will it actually feel personal?

Yes — the lyrics come from the specific moment you share about the two of you, so it's unmistakably about your story. You choose the musical style and an AI vocalist performs it. For an anniversary, that specificity is the difference between "thoughtful" and "they will cry."

How long does it take?

Under 90 seconds for a preview, about three minutes for the full 45–90 second song. You can keep re-rolling the lyrics, the beat, or the vocal until it lands — most users find the right version in two attempts.

What if our story is complicated?

Complicated is what makes the song specific. Porizo handles long-distance stories, second marriages, partners who reconnected after years apart, couples who met online, couples who met at a funeral — the more particular the detail, the better the song.

How does this compare to Songfinch or a wedding band for an anniversary?

Songfinch uses human composers, ships in around 4–7 days standard, and costs ~$179.99–$199.99 per song. A wedding band records a cover for hundreds of dollars. Porizo writes original lyrics specific to your story, previews in about 90 seconds, and delivers the full song in minutes for around $9 — instant and affordable in a way neither a composer nor a band can match.

Can both partners hear it before the anniversary?

Use the Pro plan and share the link only with yourself first to preview. Then send it on the day. Or play it together for the first time at dinner.
