The whole point

Why a song in your own voice matters.

A personalized song is already a meaningful gift. A personalized song sung in your own voice is something else entirely. The recipient hears the song, recognizes the voice, and the moment becomes a letter set to music — not a product.

This is not a stylistic upgrade. It is the difference between "thoughtful" and "I'll keep this forever." When the song is sung in a stranger's voice, the gift is the lyric. When the song is sung in your voice, the gift is you.

FAQ

Voice cloning questions.

What does "sung in your own voice" actually mean?

It means the final song's vocal track sounds like you — your tone, your inflection, your timbre — even though you didn't sing the song yourself. After a one-time voice enrollment in the app, Porizo applies AI voice conversion so that the song you create is sung in your voice, not in a stranger's.

Do I have to be a good singer?

No. Voice enrollment is recording 6–10 short phrases at a normal speaking volume, plus 1–2 simple sung prompts. You're not performing the song. The system extracts the qualities of your voice and applies them later to professionally-generated singing.

Which other personalized-song services offer this?

Among the established gift-song marketplaces — Songfinch, Songlorious, Songheart, ForeverSong — none offer voice cloning of the gifter; they use human composers and human singers. A handful of newer AI-native music apps offer voice-cloned music creation in different forms; Porizo is differentiated by being the personalized-song app built around the gift moment with voice cloning at the center.

Is my voice safe? Could it be cloned without permission?

Voice enrollment is recording-only inside the iOS app. No file uploads are accepted. Voice embeddings are encrypted with user-specific keys. Raw recordings are auto-deleted after 7 days. Impersonation detection blocks attempts to enroll a public figure's voice.

Which plans include voice cloning?

Plus ($9.99/mo) and Pro ($14.99/mo) include voice cloning. The Free plan creates one song using a default voice.

Can I gift a song using my voice for someone else to share?

Yes. The song is yours, sung in your voice, and shared as a link the recipient can play and replay. Pro plan unlocks share-with-anyone so they can forward it.
