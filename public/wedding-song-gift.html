FAQ

Wedding Song Gift questions.

How specific can the lyric get about the couple?

Very. Share the city, the date, the meal at the first date, the friend who set them up, the inside joke. Porizo writes lyrics that name the specific detail. Generic wedding songs are exactly what we are avoiding.

Will it actually feel personal?

Yes — the lyrics come from the couple's actual story, so it's unmistakably about them. You choose the musical style and an AI vocalist performs it. For a parent of the bride or groom, that specificity lands harder than any speech.

Can I play it at the reception?

Yes. Every song generates a shareable link plus a downloadable file. Hand it to the DJ, or play it from your phone during the toast.

What if the couple has a complicated story?

Complicated is what makes the song specific. Second marriages, couples who reconnected after years, couples who met online, couples who met at a funeral — the more particular the detail, the better the song.

How long does it take to make?

Preview in under 90 seconds; full song in about three minutes. Make it the morning of the wedding if needed.

Can the couple keep the song forever?

Yes. They get the link to play and replay. With share-with-anyone (Pro plan) they can post it as their first dance song or send it to friends.
