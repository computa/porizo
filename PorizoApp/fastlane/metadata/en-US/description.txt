Turn a memory, message, or special occasion into a personalized song gift.

Porizo helps you create original songs for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, thank-yous, and just-because moments. Tell us who the song is for, share a few details that matter, choose a style, and Porizo turns your words into custom lyrics and music in minutes.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Tell the story
Pick the occasion, name the person, and share a memory, message, or feeling you want the song to capture.

2. Choose the sound
Select a musical style and voice option that fits the moment.

3. Preview the song
Listen to a short preview, then keep going when it feels right.

4. Share the gift
Save the finished song in your library or send a private link your recipient can open and play.

Porizo is built for real moments: birthday surprises, anniversary memories, wedding messages, thank-you songs, family tributes, and personal gifts that feel more meaningful than another card.

No music experience needed. Start with the words you already have, and make a song they will remember.
