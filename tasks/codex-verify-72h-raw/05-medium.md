## Critical residual risks

- **M3** (NOT_FIXED): Voice biometric leak window via Suno fetch URL — even brief leak of irreversible PII to third-party logs is a privacy concern.
- **M8** (NOT_FIXED): Callback HMAC bypass via token-only auth — once token leaks (logs/proxies/referrers), replays indefinitely. Currently low-impact (route is no-op), but becomes critical the moment state mutation is added.
- **M18** (NOT_FIXED): Three sources of truth for Suno model with default mismatch (`V5` vs `V5_5`).
