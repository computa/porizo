# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Create
The heart of the app: wizard-driven flow to create personalized songs and poems — guiding users through occasion selection, story capture, and generation.

### 2. Songs
Library of all songs created, with playback controls, sharing options, and the ability to create variations.

### 3. Poems
Library of all poems created, with viewing, sharing, and variation options.

### 4. Explore
Discover occasions, templates, and inspiration — browse by category (birthdays, anniversaries, appreciation) to jumpstart creation.

### 5. Settings
Profile management, voice enrollment, notification preferences, and help/support.
