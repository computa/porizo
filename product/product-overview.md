# Porizō

> *From Ancient Greek πορίζω — "to provide, to open a way"*
> Opening the path for feelings to reach the ones you love.

## Description

A personal expression platform that helps people convey feelings that are hard to put into words — through personalized songs and poems created for loved ones. Porizō opens a way for your deepest sentiments to travel from your heart to theirs.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Deep emotions are hard to express
Words often fail when feelings run deepest. Saying "I love you" or "I'm grateful" doesn't capture the full weight of what we feel.

→ **Solution:** AI-generated songs and poems personalized with the recipient's name, your message, and the specific feelings you want to convey — giving voice to what's hard to say.

### Problem 2: Generic gifts feel impersonal
Store-bought cards and mass-produced gifts lack the intimacy of something truly personal.

→ **Solution:** Voice-cloned songs that sound like YOU singing to them. Your voice, your words, their name — an unrepeatable gift.

### Problem 3: Sharing meaningful moments feels awkward
Expressing vulnerability face-to-face can be uncomfortable, yet text messages feel too casual for deep feelings.

→ **Solution:** Private 1-on-1 sharing links that create an intimate moment. Recipients can then choose to celebrate publicly on social media — spreading joy on their terms.

### Problem 4: Creating something personal requires talent
Not everyone can write poetry or compose music, but everyone has feelings worth expressing.

→ **Solution:** AI-powered creation that transforms your raw feelings into polished songs and poems — no musical or literary talent required.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Song Generation** — Custom songs with recipient's name woven into lyrics
- **Voice Cloning** — Songs that sound like you singing (after voice enrollment)
- **Custom Poem Creation** — AI-crafted poems for any occasion or feeling
- **Private Sharing** — Secure 1-on-1 links for intimate delivery
- **Social Celebration** — Recipients can share publicly to celebrate the moment
- **Occasion Templates** — Birthdays, anniversaries, appreciation, apologies, and more
- **Story Wizard** — Guided flow to capture memories and feelings for deeper personalization
